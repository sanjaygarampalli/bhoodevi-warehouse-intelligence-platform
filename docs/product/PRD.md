# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

**Product:** BHOODEVI Warehouse Intelligence Platform (BWIP)

**Version:** 0.1.0-alpha

**Status:** Draft

**Last Updated:** July 2026

---

# Executive Summary

BHOODEVI Warehouse Intelligence Platform (BWIP) is an AI-powered Warehouse Demand Intelligence Platform that enables warehouse owners and logistics businesses to discover, qualify, manage, and convert warehouse opportunities into long-term customers.

Unlike traditional Warehouse Management Systems (WMS), BWIP focuses on generating new warehouse business through AI-driven intelligence, automation, and analytics.

---

# Business Objectives

The platform aims to:

- Increase warehouse occupancy
- Generate qualified warehouse leads
- Reduce dependence on brokers and referrals
- Automate business development activities
- Improve lead conversion
- Provide data-driven decision making
- Build a scalable SaaS platform for the warehousing industry

# Core Functional Requirements

## Lead Discovery

The system shall:

- Discover companies requiring warehouse space
- Monitor business expansion activities
- Track warehouse demand
- Identify potential customers

---

## Company Intelligence

The system shall:

- Research company profiles
- Collect business information
- Analyze warehouse requirements
- Track expansion news

---

## Decision Maker Intelligence

The system shall:

- Identify key decision-makers
- Maintain verified contact information
- Track organizational hierarchy

---

## Lead Management

The system shall:

- Create opportunities
- Track opportunity stages
- Record interactions
- Maintain communication history

---

## Proposal Management

The system shall:

- Generate warehouse proposals
- Maintain quotation history
- Track proposal status

---

## Follow-up Management

The system shall:

- Schedule follow-ups
- Send reminders
- Track pending activities

---

## Dashboard & Analytics

The system shall provide:

- Lead statistics
- Pipeline reports
- Conversion analysis
- Occupancy insights
- Business performance metrics
